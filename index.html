Chicken vs Aliens

  • Gameplay Programmer
  • Platforms: PC
  • Languages: C#
  • Tools: Unity, Kinect
  • Project page
  When Cafundo received a free stand at the Interaction South America 2017 event in Florianópolis, me and Cafundo’s creative director had a challenge to come up with some kind of interactive experience. Considering our time and what we had available we decided to use a Kinect to enable player to interact with a projection. This idea evolved into Chicken vs Aliens. A game like space invaders but that you kill the aliens by physically throwing balls at them.

  Cam-pong is a pong game where you control the bar with your hand in front of a webcam. The Rotfather

  • Gameplay Programmer
  • Platforms: PC
  • Languages: C#
  • Tools: Unity
  • Project page
  Rotfather is a transmedia project where the game is the main product. It is developed by a group from my previous university (UFSC).
  Since it was a student project we had freedom to try multiple formats of gameplay, in total we had four versions. After multiple tests we preferred to keep it as an adventure game where the player moves the character through keyboard and interact with the game using the mouse.
  This game was my undergraduate thesis where I present most of what I have learned and developed on the project.
